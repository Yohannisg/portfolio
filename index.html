const projects = [

CodeCriticAI
CodeCriticAI is a real time code review tool that gives instant suggestions inside an editor view and supports live collaboration. The goal is to reduce long review cycles and help people learn by seeing suggestions in context. The system accepts a code snippet and surrounds it with guardrails to protect private data, then asks a review engine for suggestions that focus on clarity, complexity, and logic. The interface shows inline comments, quick diffs, and a simple history so you can accept a change or roll it back with confidence. I designed the data flow for fast round trips and I added presence and cursor sync so teammates can review together. In pilots, teams reported shorter feedback loops and fewer follow up bugs.
MusicRecommender
MusicRecommender creates personal playlists by learning from listening behavior and content features. The aim is to reduce decision fatigue and help users discover songs that feel new yet familiar. The system blends patterns from plays and skips with track attributes and artist relations, then produces a ranked list that updates as you listen. A small service caches results so the app feels immediate, while an explanations pane tells you why a song appears and how to tune your taste. Early users spent more time listening and skipped fewer tracks, which suggests the blend of behavior and content works well.
StreamPulse
StreamPulse is a monitoring and analytics dashboard for continuous event data. It is built to give operators a live picture of system health and to surface unusual patterns quickly. Data flows in through stable topic design and is aggregated in short windows that update the charts in near real time. The dashboard presents a clear set of metrics, an alert banner that explains what crossed a threshold, and a simple rule editor that lets you adjust sensitivity without touching code. During demos, StreamPulse helped detect spikes and drops sooner and made it easier to explain what happened to non technical stakeholders.
TalentScanAI
TalentScanAI helps hiring teams compare resumes with job descriptions and explains the match in plain language. The platform reads a role description, maps it to skills and experience, and scores each candidate with a short rationale. Recruiters can filter by location, skills, and past roles, then open a candidate timeline to record notes and decisions. The goal is to make screening faster and more consistent while giving every decision a traceable reason. In user tests, reviewers moved through a stack of applicants more quickly and produced shortlists that matched the original requirements more closely.
StudyMateAI
StudyMateAI turns notes into practice materials and schedules review at the right time. A student uploads class notes, and the app generates flashcards, short quizzes, and summaries that focus on the key ideas.
